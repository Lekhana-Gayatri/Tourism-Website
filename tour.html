KERALA

Kerala, known as God’s own country, is one of the most popular tourist places in India. Alleppey, a city in Kerala,which is often called ‘paradise
on earth’ has many wonderful things to explore.cruising the backwaters, and living on a houseboat are some of the exciting things to do in
Alleppey. There are lot of more adventurous and fascinating things to do like boat racing. Click on explore to get the full details with pricing.




Cochin-Munnar-Thekkady-Allepey
5 Nights And 6 Days

Day 1:

Pickup from Cochin railway station/airport. Check in hotel, after fresh up visit boating, harbour, bolgatty palace, chinese fishing nets and night stay Cochin.

Day 2:

After breakfast proceed to Munnar visit tea garden point, jungle honey bee trees, go for jeep safari (optional) and night stay at Munnar.

Day 3:

After breakfast murrupetti dam, boating, film shooting point, elephanta arrival spot, eco point, night stay at Munnar.

Day 4:

After breakfast proceed to Thekkady visit boating at periyar wild life sanctuary, spice plantation shopping, night stay at thekkady

Day 5:

After breakfast proceed to Alleppey, visit Alleppey town beach, backwater ride, etc, night stay at Alleppey.

Day 6:

Alleppey to Cochin railway station/airport drop.

DELHI-AGRA-JAIPUR-AJMER

Pickup from Agra railway station, check-in at the hotel, after fresh-up, visit Taj mahal, Agra fort, and night stay at Agra.

After breakfast, proceed to Jaipur, visit Hawa mahal, city palace, ,museum, Jantar-mantar, Amber fort and night stay at Jaipur.

Day 3-

After breakfast proceeds to Ajmer sharif, ziyarat of dargah sharif and night stay at Ajmer sharif.

After breakfast, Sightseeing on own and night’s stay at Ajmer sharif.

Day 6-

After breakfast, Delhi sightseeing Jama masjid, Qutub minar, Red fort, India gate, and Delhi railway station drop.

BANGALORE-MYSORE-OOTY

Mysore-Ooty is one of the best tourist destinations to visit at least once in your life.Known for its nature and climate,Ooty is a great destination
to choose for your family vacation. The temperature in this city is usually low, between 7 to 17 degrees, which makes the city a favorite of many
people. Mysore is visited for its great monuments, museums, temples, gardens,and historic places.The palaces in Mysore are some of the most
visited monuments in the world. To get all the details of this package, click on explore.




Bangalore-Mysore-Ooty-Coonoor
3 Nights And 4 Days

Day 1-

Pick up from Bangalore railway station, check-in at hotel and after fresh-up, there will be local sightseeing in Bangalore, then we will visit Vidhan Sabha, Lal Baug, and Tipu Palace etc, and stay during the night in Bangalore.

Day 2-

Bangalore to Mysore, visit Sri Ranganathaswami temple, Tipu palace Mysore , Chamundi hills, Maharaja palace, Brindavan garden and night stay at Mysore.

Day 3-

Mysore to Ooty visit botanical garden, rose garden, Ooty lake and night stay at Ooty.

Day 4-

Coonur sightseeing, visit Sim’s park, Dolphin’s Nose, Lamb’s Rock, etc and proceed to Mysore night stay and drop.

Madurai city has many historical monuments like Meenakshi Temple and Tirumalai Nayak Palace,because of which it is visited
by many people from across India. explore more.




Madurai - Rameshwaram - Kanyakumari - Kodaikanal
3 Nights And 4 Days

Pick up from Madurai railway station, Check in hotel, night stay at Madurai.

Day 2-

Madurai Meenakshi temple darshan and proceed to Rameshwaram, visit pambam bridge ,Ramnath swami temple, 22 well bath, sea bath, Sita tirth, Laxman tirth, floating stone and proceed to Kanyakumari night stay.
